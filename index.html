Introduction

Xiori is my dog. Xiori was rescued from construction remains and we adopted her on May 2023. She is now almost two years old.

Background

Her name was difficult to come up with. As we never really tought of having a dog, there were not many options to choose from, and she remained nameless for the first couple of days. Finally, my mom came up with the name of Xiori, which comes from the Japanese name "Shiori" and means "Guidance".

Personality

Xio likes to play a lot, but sometimes she goes crazy and might leave a scratch or two. She has catched some habits that make her unique, such as:

  1. Rip her bed
  2. Break her toys
  3. Ask for food even if she is full
  4. Bark at anything
  5. Cry if you don't give her food
